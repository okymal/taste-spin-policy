Account Deletion Request — Taste Spin

If you have created an account in the Taste Spin app using your Google account and wish to permanently delete it, please follow the steps below.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to okymal111@gmail.com with the subject line “Delete my Taste Spin account”.
  2. Be sure to write from the same Google account that you used to sign in to the app, so we can verify ownership.
  3. We will confirm your request and permanently delete your account and all associated data within 7 days.

What data will be deleted

  • All user-created dish lists and custom food data stored in Firebase.
  • All identifiers linked to your Google account used in the app.

No other data (such as contacts, photos, or location) is collected or stored by Taste Spin.

What data will be retained

We do not retain any personal data after deletion. Only anonymized statistical information (if available in Firebase analytics) may remain for internal security and stability purposes.

After deletion

If you reinstall the app later, you can sign in again with your Google account to create a new profile from scratch.

For any questions or concerns, please contact us at okymal111@gmail.com.
